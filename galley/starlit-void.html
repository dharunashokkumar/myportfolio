About Me

[ a.k.a. who is StarlitVoid?? ]

webmistress
self-portrait
(150×150)

Hey! The name's Jess but online everybody calls me StarlitVoid (long story, involves a screensaver). I taught myself HTML the summer of '99 from a library book and I've been making websites ever since. By day I'm a high-school junior; by night I'm rendering spheres on a checkerboard floor and waiting 40 minutes for them to finish.

My dream is to make backgrounds for video games someday. For now I practice in Bryce 3D and a cracked copy of 3D Studio my cousin gave me. I also collect MIDIs, hoard desktop wallpapers, and run three webrings (badly).
