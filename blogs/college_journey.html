My LPU Journey: From JEE Struggles to a New Beginning

The past year has been an emotional rollercoaster — from late-night study sessions to unexpected results, endless counseling rounds, and finally landing at Lovely Professional University. This post documents how I turned rejection into redirection, and why I chose to be the head of a cat rather than the tail of a tiger.

JEE: The Challenge That Started It All

Like millions of Indian aspirants, I aimed for top engineering colleges through JEE Main. I joined Sri Abhida Junior College, Hyderabad, and gave my best despite ups and downs.

When the results came, I had a few good options — top IIITs like Kalyani, Dharwad, and Vadodara, but only for branches like Mechanical and Civil.

I knew I wanted Computer Science Engineering (CSE). That was my passion, my future.

So I took a bold decision: "Better to be the head of a cat than the tail of a tiger." – I chose what aligned with my goals, not just what looked good on paper.

JoSAA & CSAB Counseling Rounds

Here's how the counseling journey went:

JoSAA Counseling:

- Allotted: IIITs with Mech/Civil branches

- Why I skipped: Not aligned with my core interest in tech

CSAB Special Rounds:

- Gave it one last try — still no ideal match

- Final realization: It's not about just getting in, it's about what you'll do after getting in

Instead of settling, I decided to create my own path.

Why I Chose Lovely Professional University (LPU)

LPU wasn't my first thought — but it became my best decision:

- Beautiful campus and strong digital infrastructure

- CSE program with focus on AI/ML, Cybersecurity, and Python

- Active placement cell and industry-aligned projects

- Freedom to build and experiment – startups, side projects, hackathons

- Supportive environment that values both academics and innovation

LPU didn't just give me a seat — it gave me a stage.

My First Year Plan

I've already mapped out my learning journey:

- Mastering Python (official course language)

- Learning C and C++ independently

- Strengthening DSA skills

- Practicing on LeetCode, Codeforces

- Dreaming big — MNC placements, internships, and product development

My Projects & Startups

Even before joining college, I've been building my own ecosystem:

DadsCloud: My self-hosted data center project — India's local AWS alternative

TripleETech: A startup aimed at IT services, electronics, automation, AI & web development

Open-Source Contributions: From Devicon to custom AI assistants — building with the community

Personal Branding: Sharing my journey through my blog, YouTube channel [pop2all], and my portfolio

LPU gives me the environment to scale these dreams — with labs, mentors, and like-minded peers.

Lessons I've Learned

This journey taught me a lot more than books ever could:

- Failure is just redirection

- Focus on your goal, not trends or rank lists

- Your path is your power — own it

Choosing LPU over a "tag" college wasn't a compromise — it was a commitment to my CSE passion and startup dream.

The Road Ahead

Now that I'm here, the real journey begins:

- I'll build tools, platforms, and businesses from my hostel room

- I'll collaborate, contribute, and grow

- I'll be the best version of me — at my own pace, on my own terms

If you're someone who's stuck between "what you got" vs "what you want" — remember this: "Be the head of a cat, rather than the tail of a tiger." Choose growth, not just glory.
