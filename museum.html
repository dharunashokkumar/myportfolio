internet museum by dharun

an interactive museum preserving internet history, culture, and the moments that shaped how we live online. from the dancing baby to ai agents, from geocities to tiktok, this is where internet culture lives forever.

curatorial approach: exhibits selected for cultural impact, technical significance, or pure nostalgia value. organized into thematic "wings" inspired by neal.fun's internet artifacts and the internet hall of fame.
