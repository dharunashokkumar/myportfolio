what it is

cs archive is a free, multi-page knowledge hub for computer science students and self-taught developers. high information density, a retro web-1.0 look, and quick access to curated resources across 20+ categories — skills and roadmaps, courses, free services, self-hosted tools, top repos, learning paths, ai models, tutorials, research papers, project ideas, careers, dev tools, certifications, communities, cheatsheets, public apis, security and ctf labs, design, newsletters, nostalgia, and awesome-lists.

content is json-driven and the whole thing is a static site — no backend, github-pages compatible. it reached version 3.0.0 before i archived it.
